About Invest Education:

The purpose of Invest Education is to help people learn about investments through providing both useful and informative investment tools. Currently the only implemented tool allows the user to inspect a specified stock value over the limited span of time that our stock data covers. Future tools of Invest Education include a stock comparison tool and a stock ROI tool displaying different investment strategies.
